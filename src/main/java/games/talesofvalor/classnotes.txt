Healer
- Heal: Heal x health -> friendly target
- Life Tap: Take damage and draw an additional card for a target -> self & friendly targets
Tank
- Taunt: Redirect incoming attacks to you -> self cast (implemented)
- Dazzle: Skip the target's turn -> hostile target
Combatant:
- Cleave: Deal attack damage to all targets -> all hostile targets nearby
- Empower: Empower a target's next attack to do 25% increased damage -> self & friendly targets

POIs:
- Shrine of ressurection -> Ressurect a dead player
- Shrine of Stats -> The player gets +2 to their primary stat, others get +1.
- Jester -> Draws a card for all players
